Our Infrastructure

Fully Equipped State-of-the-Art Facilities

At Dr. Karpe's Centre for Advanced Dentistry, we strive to deliver the highest level of dental care through state-of-the-art clinical infrastructure. Every corner of our clinic is optimized for hygiene, safety, and patient convenience.

Our facility is organized into four separate clinical divisions, enabling specialists to focus on specific procedures under one single roof.

OPD Unit
General dental checkups and primary diagnostics.
Surgical Unit
Advanced sterile theatre for implantology and surgeries.
Child Unit
Welcoming environment tailored specifically for pediatric care.
In-house OPG Unit
Digital panoramic orthopantomogram X-rays for instant scans.

Hygiene protocol compliant with American standards.

Our Specialists

Meet Our Experienced Doctors

Our specialists coordinate diagnosis and planning to deliver optimal aesthetic and restorative treatment outcomes.

Implantology Specialist

Dr. Vivek Karpe

Dr. Vivek Karpe completed his BDS from the prestigious Government Dental College, Mumbai and has over 17 years of clinical experience. He specializes in dental implants and has successfully performed hundreds of procedures. He trained in the advanced "All-on-4" dental implant concept in Germany and regularly attends international symposiums.

BDS (GDC Mumbai) ITI Member ICOI Member Germany Trained
Cosmetic Dentistry Specialist

Dr. Ashwini Karpe

Dr. Ashwini Karpe completed her BDS from the Government Dental College, Aurangabad. She has been practicing exclusively in cosmetic dentistry for over 13 years. With an exceptional aesthetic eye, she has delivered beautiful smiles to many patients using porcelain laminates, veneers, and modern teeth whitening protocols.

BDS (GDC Aurangabad) IAACD Member Cosmetic Specialist Smile Makeovers
